AeroRouteMap Support

AeroRouteMap is an iOS flight-planning reference and route visualization app.

Before reporting a data issue

  • AeroRouteMap is not certified navigation data and is not for operational navigation.
  • Waypoint and navaid data can be incomplete, stale, or different from current official publications.
  • Airway segment geometry is incomplete until an authoritative licensed airway database is connected.

What to include

When reporting a route or waypoint issue, include the route text, the waypoint name, the expected coordinate or official reference, and the AeroRouteMap NavData cycle shown in the app.

Contact

Open a support issue at https://github.com/fox3nova/aeroroutemap/issues. If GitHub Issues are unavailable, contact the developer through the App Store product page.

Privacy
